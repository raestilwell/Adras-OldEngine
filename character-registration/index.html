New Character

In Adrastium, players are granted the opportunity to forge three unique characters, one of which can belong to a custom race of their own creation, provided it has been submitted and approved on our Discord server.

Your character name must be 3-15 characters long and may only contain letters.
Character Progression Notice

For fair and balanced roleplay, all characters in Adrastium begin with baseline class abilities and progress through consistent, positive roleplay contributions. This honor system ensures everyone has an enjoyable experience while their character grows naturally in the world.

Through your journey in Adrastium, your character will develop skills, abilities, and connections that reflect your roleplay choices and community contributions.

Legacy Character Progression System

Due to the aetherial anomalies in Adrastium, your legacy character will initially revert to their basic class abilities. We operate on an honor system for fair roleplay among all players.

Character Progression Tiers:
  • Tier 1 (Minimum 30 days): Basic class abilities only
  • Tier 2: Limited special abilities unlocked after demonstrating good roleplay
  • Tier 3 (Minimum 3+ months): More significant abilities unlocked after consistent, positive contribution to the community

An administrator will reach out to discuss your character's journey within Adrastium's setting.
