Adrastium Commands List

Interface Commands


-/+ooc
Turns on and off ooc announcement channel.

-/+map
Hides the floating directional map visible onscreen

-/+friend player
Adds the indicated player to your friends list

-/+ignore player
Adds the indicated player to your ignore list

look direction
Allows the player to look in the specified direction (north, west, east, south) to see which players can be found on the tiles in that direction.

settings
Allows the player to customize their interface settings. Player must log in again for changes to take effect.

help
Pulls up the In-Game Help screen in a new tab

quit
Logs the player out of the game.


Movement

s/south
move South

e/east
move East

w/west
move West

u/up
move up

d/down

Advanced Movement

sethome
Sets the recall of the player to whatever room it is posted in. This can be reset to another location at any time.

recall
Your character is instantly teleported back to the location they have set as their home.

follow character
Your character shadows the movement of the selected character, appearing on whatever tile they click onto until the 'follow' command is typed.


Communication


say
Public speech directed to everyone in the room you are currently in; ex. say Hello. appears as You said, "Hello." or John said, "Hello." to other players.
Additionally, you can use the dropdown to the right of the submit bar to change the language your character is speaking in. This applies to say, say to, shout, and whisper to commands. Only other characters who also understand the language will be able to see what was said. ex. with Elvish selected say to Sela Hello. Appears as You said in Elvish, "Hello.", John said something in Elvish., or John said in Elvish, "Hello."

say to character
Public speech directed toward one person in the room you are currently occupying; ex. say to Sela Hello. Appears as You said to Sela, "Hello." or John said to Sela, "Hello."

shout
Public speech that will reach everyone in Adrastium (Crazy, right?); ex. shout Hello! Appears as You shouted, "Hello!" or John shouted, "Hello!" to other players.

tell to character
Sends a private message to the indicated character anywhere in game. Can be implemented for private ooc tells, as well as ic communication.

whisper to character
Sends a private message to the indicated character that is presently in the same room.

ooc
Public, ooc message made to everyone logged into Adrastium.

/
Displays the action that your character performed and is visible to all in your room; ex. /waved. Appears as John waved. A space will have to be added after the / character.

read
Reads a list, board, sign, note or etc, that is available in the room; ex. read list

post
Places a message on a public board


Guild/Clan/Militia Commands


Guild Commands

+/-guild character
Guild Leader only, adds or kicks the selected player from the guild.

guild
Allows guild members to chat on a private channel.

Clan Commands

+/-clan character
Clan Leader only, adds or kicks the selected player from the clan.

clan
Allows clan members to chat on a private channel.

Militia Commands

+/-militia character
Militia Leader only, adds or kicks the selected player from the militia.

militia
Allows militia members to chat on a private channel.
